About Me

Hello! My name is Emma, feel free to call me Em. By day, I am a Front Desk Agent. I smile and make visits to Tucson memorable for everyone I enteract with. By night, I am Mom to 3 amazing boys. We enjoy dance parties (the Cha Cha slide is our jam), junk food and movie night, all day outings to the park and working in the garden.
